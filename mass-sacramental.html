Sunday Worship & Church Services

At Jesus Christ Compassion Ministries Church, our worship services are a time to honor God, grow spiritually, and strengthen our fellowship as a family of believers. Every gathering is centered on the Word, heartfelt praise, and the moving of the Holy Spirit.

  • Sunday Worship: Experience powerful praise, uplifting sermons, and moments of prayer that draw us closer to God.
  • Water Baptism: A public declaration of faith and new life in Christ for those ready to take the next step.
  • Marriage Blessings & Counseling: Guidance, prayer, and support for couples preparing for or strengthening their marriage.
  • Prayer & Deliverance: Special moments of ministry for healing, restoration, and spiritual breakthrough.

Whether you're visiting for the first time, returning to faith, or looking for a home church where you can grow and serve, our doors and hearts are open to welcome you.

For service schedules, baptism preparation, or more information about our ministries, feel free to contact us or visit the Contact Page. We look forward to worshipping with you.
