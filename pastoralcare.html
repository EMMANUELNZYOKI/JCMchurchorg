Caring for Body, Mind, and Spirit

Our Pastoral Care Ministry is dedicated to supporting individuals and families through every season of life. Through compassion, prayer, and personal connection, our ministers and volunteers offer comfort, guidance, and practical care to those in need.

  • Hospital & Home Visits: Bringing prayer, encouragement, and Christ-centered support to the sick, elderly, and homebound.
  • Bereavement Support: Offering comfort, assistance with funeral arrangements, and continued care for families walking through grief.
  • Spiritual Counseling: Providing confidential guidance, prayer, and encouragement for personal, emotional, or family struggles.
  • Prayer Ministry: Interceding for the needs of individuals, families, and the church through dedicated prayer teams and weekly gatherings.
  • Pastoral Support: Helping individuals draw closer to God during times of crisis, illness, or transition.

At Jesus Christ Compassion Ministries Church, no one should walk alone. Whether you are facing sickness, loss, or simply need someone to pray with you, our Pastoral Care team is here to stand with you.

To request a visit or learn more about serving in this ministry, please contact the church office or speak with one of our pastors after service.
